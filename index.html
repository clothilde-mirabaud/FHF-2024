History

The FHF was founded in 2004 by a group of 15 friends, who wanted to play football together and enter competitions. They created the first traditions, such as surnames, songs and even chose the colors of the jersey, that are still the same nowadays !

Our biggest project : Women European Football Tournament (WEFT)

Last year, we launched the first edition of the WEFT, with 6 teams coming from everywhere in Europe (England, Sweden...). It was a real pleasure to meet them all and get to know them, as well as compete against them

Our record

We are in the top level university League, and we competed last year in the National Cup for universities. We finished second, so we are now vice-champion of France, and we aim to do better next year !
